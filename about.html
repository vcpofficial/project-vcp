About VCP

Welcome to VCP Website

There is no need to be confused because VCP is a user-friendly website. Basically, it records your voice or speech and creates a pdf. Using this website, taking notes is simple. Students used services like Zoom, Google Meet, and others during COVID 19, which had such a severe pandemic. We have seen that occasionally, students are unable to take down notes on what their teachers are saying. We have provided a useful resource that can assist you with this issue. It is beneficial to everyone, not just students.
